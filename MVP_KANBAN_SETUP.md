## Recommended Workflow

### Daily Standup (5 min)
1. Open **ðŸŽ¯ MVP Kanban**
2. Review **In Progress** - what's blocked?
3. Move completed items to **Done**
4. Pull new items from **Todo** to **In Progress**

### Sprint Planning (30 min)
1. Review **Backlog** column
2. Move high-priority items to **Todo**
3. Assign team members
4. Set realistic goals for the sprint

### Weekly Review (15 min)
1. Check completion rate
2. Identify bottlenecks (too many in one column?)
3. Re-prioritize if needed
